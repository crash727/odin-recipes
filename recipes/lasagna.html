lasagna

Cook ground beef and sweet italian sausage together with garlic,salt,and pepper. Boil lasagna noodles. after sausage and ground are cooked drain grease. now layer your dish starting with noodles then thin layer of sauce , followed by ricotta cheese. then noodles thin layer of sauce then meat with shredded mozzerlla cheese on top . Then noodles and i put whatever i have left as 3rd layer which can be a mixture of meat and cheese. folloed by noodles and sauce on top covered by shredded cheese and wrap in foil and put in oven at 375 degrees for 1 hour.

Ingredients

  • ground beef
  • ground sausage
  • shredded mozzerela
  • ricotta cheese
  • pasta sauce
  • garlic
  • lasagna noodles

Steps

  1. Cook lasagna noodles
  2. Cook ground beef and drain grease
  3. Add garlic powder, salt, and pepper to ground beef
  4. Start with some sauce on bottom of pan layer of noodles on sauce
  5. Then ricotta cheese or ground beef
  6. Then noodles followed by which ever you prefer
  7. Add sauce and shredded mozzerella to each layer as you build
  8. When built noodles should be on top
  9. On top of noodles add some sauce topped with shredded mozerella
  10. Wrap with foil
  11. Cook 45 mins on 375
  12. Cook for additional 15 mins without foil
